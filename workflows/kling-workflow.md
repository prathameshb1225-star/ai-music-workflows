# Kling AI Music Video Workflow

## Objective

To create a cinematic AI-generated music video using structured prompts and motion control.

---

## Step 1: Scene Planning

Break the song into segments:

* Intro (0:00–0:20) → Establish mood
* Verse (0:20–0:50) → Character + environment
* Chorus (0:50–1:20) → Emotional peak
* Verse 2 (1:20–1:50) → Narrative expansion
* Outro (1:50–2:30) → Resolution

---

## Step 2: Prompt Mapping

Assign each scene a prompt:

* Rain walking → loneliness
* Window scene → reflection
* Flashback → contrast emotion
* Rooftop → isolation
* Sunrise → closure

Consistency is critical:
Use the same character description across prompts.

---

## Step 3: Generate Base Clips (Kling)

* Input prompts into Kling AI
* Use motion control:

  * “slow tracking shot”
  * “camera orbit”
  * “zoom in”
* Generate multiple variations per scene

---

## Step 4: Clip Selection

Reject:

* inconsistent faces
* unnatural motion
* low realism

Select only clips that:

* match tone
* maintain character consistency
* feel cinematic

---

## Step 5: Editing (Runway or similar)

* Arrange clips according to timeline
* Add transitions (fade, cross dissolve)
* Sync visuals with lyrics timing
* Adjust speed for emotional impact

---

## Step 6: Sound Integration

* Add background score or song
* Align beat drops with visual changes
* Maintain pacing

---

## Step 7: Final Polish

* Color grading (cool tones for sadness, warm for flashbacks)
* Add grain or film texture
* Export in high quality

---

## Key Insight

AI tools generate clips.
You create the film.

The difference is:

* prompt quality
* selection discipline
* editing decisions
